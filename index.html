CRITICAL

Never Share OTP Codes

Your OTP and login codes are confidential. Never share them — no legitimate bank, service provider, or official representative will request them.

WARNING

Verify Before Sending Money

Be cautious of unexpected EasyPaisa or JazzCash requests from unknown numbers. Always verify identity before making payments.

ALERT

Avoid Suspicious Links

Avoid unknown links shared through WhatsApp, Facebook, or SMS claiming prizes, free packages, or rewards.

SECURITY

Avoid Fake Applications

Install apps only from trusted sources and avoid modified APK files that may contain security risks.

PROTECT

Secure Your Accounts

Use strong passwords, enable security settings, and review account activity regularly.

AWARENESS

Use Public WiFi Carefully

Avoid sensitive transactions on unknown public networks and verify network security before connecting.

About SafePK Portal

Our Vision

To empower every Pakistani internet user with cybersecurity awareness, helping them protect digital accounts, mobile wallets, and personal information from online threats.

What We Check

To help users identify and avoid common cyber scams including fake prize calls, OTP fraud, fake WhatsApp verification attempts, phishing links, and account takeover risks before damage happens.

Your Privacy

All security checks are designed to work privately inside your browser. SafePK does not send passwords or security test data to external servers. Your information stays on your own device during normal usage.

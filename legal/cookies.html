Cookies Policy

LAST REVISED: JUNE 2026

This policy explains how SafePK uses browser-based storage technologies to provide a better user experience.

1. Cookies Usage

SafePK does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies for collecting user activity.

2. Browser Local Storage

SafePK may use browser local storage to save selected preferences or application settings. This information remains stored on your device and is managed by your browser.

3. Data Handling

SafePK does not operate a backend database for user accounts. Information entered into tools is processed within the application environment and is not sent to external servers unless a separate service is introduced.

4. Managing Storage

Users can remove local storage data at any time through their browser settings.

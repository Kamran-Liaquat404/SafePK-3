Privacy Policy

LAST REVISED: JUNE 2026

This policy explains how SafePK handles user information and browser-based data while using the platform.

1. Local Processing

SafePK tools are designed to process security checks within your browser environment. Password checks and generated content are handled locally and are not stored by SafePK servers.

2. Browser Storage

SafePK may use browser localStorage to save selected settings or preferences. This information stays on your device and can be removed through browser settings.

3. No Account Collection

SafePK does not require user accounts and does not maintain a backend database for storing personal profiles.

4. External References

Any external links or reporting references provided through SafePK are for informational purposes and are managed by their respective organizations.
